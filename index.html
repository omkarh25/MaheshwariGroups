About Maheshwari Industrial Supplies

Maheshwari Industrial Supplies is a leading manufacturer & supplier of all types of accessories and hardware goods suitable for manufacturing different range of control panels in India. Our wide ambit of reliable products include Control Panel Accessories such as Acoustic Enclosure Locks and Hinges, 3 Point Locks, Mechanical Interlock Hinges, Rubber Gaskets, Sleeves, F.R.P. & Hylam Sheets, Filtering and Drawing Pouches, etc.

We are gifted with deep industry knowledge and vast experience of the field and apply the same at every stage to come up with innovative accessories which are precisely suitable for various applications.
